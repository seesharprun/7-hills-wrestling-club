# FAQ

## Is this only for advanced wrestlers?

No. We support beginners through experienced athletes with grouped instruction.

## What should a new athlete bring?

Athletic clothing, wrestling shoes if available, water, and a consistent mindset.

## How often should I train?

For most athletes, 2-3 quality sessions each week creates solid progress.

## How do I get started?

Use the contact page and we will help place the athlete in the right program.
