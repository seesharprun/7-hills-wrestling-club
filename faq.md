# FAQ

## How much does it cost?

Nothing beyond a USA Wrestling card. There are no club dues, no gear fees, and no hidden costs. Our goal is to keep wrestling completely free for every RPS student.

## Who can join?

Any current Richmond Public Schools student. The club is open to every experience level — first-timers and veteran wrestlers are equally welcome.

## How often do you practice?

We meet once a month on a Thursday, rotating between John Marshall, Huguenot, Richmond High School for the Arts, and Armstrong. Each school hosts one session per month.

## What should I bring?

Athletic clothing, wrestling shoes if you have them (they are not required), a water bottle, and a willingness to learn. We will provide everything else.

## What does a typical session look like?

Sessions focus on technique and drilling — stance, motion, takedowns, and mat work. We also run controlled live situations so athletes can apply what they learn. The emphasis is on development, not conditioning.

## Do I need prior wrestling experience?

Not at all. Our sessions are designed for every skill level. Coaches group instruction so beginners get the fundamentals they need while experienced wrestlers continue to grow.

## How do I sign up?

Head to the [contact page](/contact/) and send us a message. We will share the next practice date and everything you need to get started.
